CONCRETE PUMP

These are hydraulic pumps that are mounted on to a truck. Using various connected steel pipes and hoses, the concrete is pumped at speed to a precise location. These pumps can reach up to 150m long, so even the most difficult of access routes can be overcome and the concrete can be delivered swiftly.
